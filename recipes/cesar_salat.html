Cesar salat

Description

Caesar Salad is an internationally known salad attributed to Italian-American Cesare Cardini.
The main ingredient is romaine lettuce served with a special dressing and croutons and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 large head of romaine lettuce
  • shredded parmesan cheese
  • crisp croutons
  • caesar salad dressing

Steps

  1. Rinse, dry and chop or tear the romaine into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Place in a large serving bowl and sprinkle generously with shredded parmesan cheese and cooled croutons.
  3. Drizzle with caesar dressing and toss gently until lettuce is evenly coated.
